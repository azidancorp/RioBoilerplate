TextInputFocusEvent

Holds information regarding a text input focus event.

This is a simple dataclass that stores useful information for when a
[`TextInput`](/docs/api/textinput) gains or loses focus. You'll typically receive this as argument
in `on_gain_focus` and `on_lose_focus` events.

Attributes

text
`str`

The `text` of the [`TextInput`](/docs/api/textinput).
