MultiLineTextInputFocusEvent

Holds information regarding a [`MultiLineTextInput`](/docs/api/multilinetextinput) focus event.

This is a simple dataclass that stores useful information for when a
[`MultiLineTextInput`](/docs/api/multilinetextinput) gains or loses focus. You'll typically receive this as
argument in `on_gain_focus` and `on_lose_focus` events.

Attributes

text
`str`

The `text` of the [`MultiLineTextInput`](/docs/api/multilinetextinput).
