DrawerOpenOrCloseEvent

Holds information regarding a drawer open or close event.

This is a simple dataclass that stores useful information for when the user
opens or closes a drawer. You'll typically receive this as an argument in
[`on_open_or_close`](/docs/api/drawer) events.

Attributes

is_open
`bool`

The new `is_open` state of the [`Drawer`](/docs/api/drawer).
