FrostedGlassFill
Fills a shape with a frosted glass effect.

FrostedGlassFill fills the shape with a color and applies a blur effect to the background, creating a frosted glass appearance.

Make sure to use a color with transparency, otherwise it will look like a SolidFill.

Constructor
FrostedGlassFill(
    color: rio.Color, 
    blur_size: float = 0.3, 
)
